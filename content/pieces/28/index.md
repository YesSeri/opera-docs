Masetto is furious with Zerlina because she almost was seduced by Don Giovanni. In this aria Zerlina tries to convince Masetto that she is innocent. 

She tells him - beat me if you want, I will stand here meek as a lamb. Tear my hair and I will still gladly kiss you. Let us be friends again, because I am innocent.
